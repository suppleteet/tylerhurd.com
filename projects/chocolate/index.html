Chocolate

Chocolate is a 3-4 minute standing room-scale VR music video experience for the HTC Vive & Oculus Rift.

Presented by Viacom NEXT and set to the song by Giraffage of the same name, Chocolate drops you as a robot into a world of colorful chrome in which everything is heavily synchronized to the music. Your hands transform to cannons that spray cute chrome cats in slow motion to a tribe of people that are performing a ritualistic dance just for you, their robot god, to provide them with their precious resource, the cute lil' shiny kitties. Your hands transform into pulse speakers that shoot giant shock waves out into the vast deforming chrome landscape. There's also a herd of giant cat heads in migrating in the distance & a massive tribal chief cat that assists in the ritual by spraying champagne all over you in slow motion like a rap video. Everything is shiny and glimmering, smooth and sexy, cute and strange, overwhelming and astonishing.
