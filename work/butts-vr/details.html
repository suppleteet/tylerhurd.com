Overview

BUTTS started as a solo animation project by Tyler Hurd, a 2 minute animated short film released in early 2014, made without VR in mind. Immediately following Skillman & Hackett got involved and it was converted to a VR experience for the DK1. Storytelling moments were creatively reimagined for the new medium. Recognized by many as the first animated short film in VR, it was nominated for Most Innovative at The Proto Awards in 2014, and since has been released on Gear VR, HTC Vive, and Oculus Rift.
