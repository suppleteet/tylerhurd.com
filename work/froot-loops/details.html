Overview

Nathan Love takes on the Kellogg’s® Froot Loops® campaign with a 3D overhaul of the legendary Toucan Sam and his beloved Nephews! “Carl the King Crab” introduces a brand new villain, in all his detailed glory, to the already memorable and fun lineup of Kellogg’s® Froot Loops® villain history.

Contribution

Previs and camera layout pass. Animation for Toucan Sam and his nephews on several shots. Rigged props.
