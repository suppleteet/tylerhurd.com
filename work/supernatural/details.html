Overview

Supernatural is a premium, subscription-based virtual reality (VR) fitness service developed by Within and owned by Meta, designed exclusively for the Meta Quest headset platform. It focuses on providing a high-intensity, full-body cardio workout while transporting users to photorealistic, 3D-captured locations around the world.
