Overview

Uncle Ben's "Teach Your Children" is a 3d animated spot created by Hornet Inc. for the Ben's Beginners campaign. Director Gabe Askew and the CG team created a beautiful, handmade world showing how children pay attention to all their parents habits, both good and bad. In this commercial, Dad teaches his son many things including how to eat healthy.

Contribution

Rigging and animation for all characters in the commercial.
